Shri S.Ramaswamy Pillai Higher secondary school is Goverment aided co-educated school in a small village Ilanji in Tirunelveli District, close to Courtallam. It was started in 1916 as primary school. We are proud to celebrate our centenary this year.

At present 1800 students are studying from 6th standard to 12th standard in our school. It is a Government aided and co-educated school, with 60 teaching staff are working in our school and having 40 class rooms in 8 acres of land.

School was started in 1916 by Shri I .C Akilandam Pillai as primary school and in 1918 was upgraded into a middle school. In 1921 Shri S.Ramaswamy Pillai donated land and building to the school and was renamed after him ever since. In 1979 it was upgraded to higher secondary school.
